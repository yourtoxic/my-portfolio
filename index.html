Who Am I? 🤔

I'm an Electrical & Electronic Engineering student at Sylhet Engineering College (SEC), Bangladesh. Beyond circuits and power systems, I'm deeply passionate about the digital frontier.

My journey into cybersecurity started with curiosity — wondering how systems could be broken and, more importantly, how to defend them. Today, I actively participate in bug bounty programs, practice ethical hacking, and build robots that bridge the physical and digital worlds.

I believe in learning by doing, breaking things to understand them, and sharing knowledge with the community. When I'm not hunting bugs or coding, you'll find me tinkering with microcontrollers or exploring new attack surfaces.
